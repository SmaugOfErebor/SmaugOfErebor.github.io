October 8, 2024 - Publication

TP-255 Issue 2: G-PON & XG-PON & XGS-PON Interoperability Test Plan

The culmination of a few years of work in the Fiber Access Networks work area of the Broadband Forum through the University of New Hampshire Interoperability Laboratory. TP-255 Issue 2 is a test plan used to verify the interoperability of an Optical Line Termination (OLT) and an Optical Network Unit (ONU).

This test plan is based primarily on four other specifications:
1. The International Telecommunication Union Telecommunication Standardization Sector's (ITU-T) G.988 ONU management and control interface (OMCI) specification.
2. The Broadband Forum's TR-101 Issue 2: Migration to Ethernet-Based Broadband Aggregation.
3. The Broadband Forum's TR-156 Issue 4: Using GPON Access in the context of TR-101.
4. The Broadband Forum's TR-280 Issue 2: ITU-T PON in the Context of TR-178.
