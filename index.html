Xylem and Phloem

Play as Xylem, a tree trying to outgrow the evil tree Phloem. Grow your roots to gather nutrients. Grow your leaves to convert nutrients to chlorophyll. Win by severing the base branch or root of Phloem. Made with Godot Engine in under 72 hours for Ludum Dare with the help of my friend CodeNMore.


Snuffles

A 2D platformer where you play as a boy that gains or loses abilities as a result of what smells he has encountered. Currently in development using Unity.
